Your Roadmap

The 5-Step Buying Process

01

Get Pre-Approved

Start with pre-approval. It tells you what you can afford, helps you shop with real numbers, and shows sellers you're serious. If you don't have a lender yet, I can point you toward a few trusted options.

02

Find Your Guide

A trusted REALTOR® makes the process much easier. I'll help with strategy, paperwork, negotiations, and the small details that can get confusing fast.

03

Start Your Search

This is where it gets real. We tour homes, compare neighborhoods, talk through tradeoffs, and keep looking until the right place starts to feel obvious.

04

Make the Offer

When you're ready, we write the offer and I negotiate for you. Price matters, but so do timing, repairs, contingencies, and the details that protect you.

05

Close & Celebrate

Closing day is the fun part. You sign, get the keys, and walk into a home that's finally yours. And yes, we celebrate.
