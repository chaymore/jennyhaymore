Hello!

Let me introduce myself.

My name is Jenny Haymore and I am a mother of four amazing children, the wife of a wonderful man, and passionate about helping my clients have successful transactions. And not just successful transactions, but HAPPY ones! I believe in win-win and that by working together we can make the process of buying and selling your home not only smooth, but also positive. Your happiness is my priority!

With over 10 years of real estate experience, a business management degree, and experience with transactions involving residential, rental, and commercial properties, I know what it takes to buy and sell homes. I know what it feels like to be a buyer and seller and a landlord. Real estate can feel like a roller coaster ride--Ups and Downs--I know this ride!

I can't wait to put my skills and experience to work for you--whether preparing your home for the market or helping you find your new home. I listen carefully, I plan accordingly, and I act intentionally...all for YOUR successful transaction.

Reach out to me for a no-cost consultation and you'll see that I am here to help you with your Happy Home!

What Sets Me Apart

What I Bring to Every Transaction

01

Community

Growing up in a small town in northern Utah, I learned early what community really means. I served as Youth City Council Mayor, organizing events and service projects. That same love for community shapes how I serve Edmond and greater Oklahoma City today. I want to help you find a house, but I also want you to feel at home in the neighborhood.

02

A Sense for Business

My Business Management degree is backed by years of practical experience, and honestly, managing a household of six has been its own MBA. I'll put those coordination skills to work for you by managing timelines, deadlines, and all the moving pieces so you always know what's next.

03

A Sense for You

I truly love people and want what's best for them. Integrity isn't a selling point to me. It's the foundation of any real relationship. Being genuine is my best practice in life, and that carries directly into how I show up for my clients. You'll always know where things stand.
