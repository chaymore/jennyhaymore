Your Game Plan

8 Tips for a Successful Sale

01

Set the Right Price

  • Look at recent sales of similar homes in your area
  • Use a comparative market analysis to choose a smart price
  • Overpricing discourages buyers — underpricing leaves money on the table
02

Enhance Curb Appeal

  • Mow the lawn, trim bushes, and add fresh flowers
  • Clean gutters, power wash siding, and touch up paint where needed
  • A new doormat and updated outdoor lighting go a long way
03

Stage Your Home

  • Remove personal items and excess furniture to make spaces feel larger
  • Stick to neutral colors to appeal to the widest range of buyers
  • Arrange furniture to highlight key features — fireplace, windows, open floor plan
04

Professional Photography

  • High-quality photos matter because most buyers start online
  • Ensure every room is clean, decluttered, and well-lit before the shoot
  • Great photos are the first showing — make them count
05

Write a Compelling Listing

  • Highlight your home's most attractive features and the neighborhood
  • Use search-friendly language: "updated kitchen," "spacious backyard"
  • Tell a story — paint a picture of what it's like to actually live there
06

Maximize Online Exposure

  • Your home will be listed on the MLS so active buyers can find it
  • Social media promotion on Facebook and Instagram to reach more buyers
  • Virtual tours give remote buyers a detailed, immersive view of the home
07

Prepare for Showings

  • Stay flexible — accommodate buyers' schedules when possible
  • Keep the home consistently clean and welcoming during the listing period
  • Arrange for pets to be out of the home during showings
08

Stay Open to Offers

  • Be open to negotiation and take every offer seriously
  • Responding promptly keeps buyers engaged and deals moving
  • Lean on me for guidance on handling offers — that's what I'm here for
